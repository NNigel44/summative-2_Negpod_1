Simple Recipe Omelette

An easy and quick dish, perfect for any meal. This classic Omelette combines beaten eggs cooked to perfection, optionally filled with your choice of cheese, vegetables, or meats.

Preparation time

  • Preparation: 5 minutes
  • Coocking: 5 minutes
  • Total time: 10 minutes

Ingredients

  • 2-3 large eggs
  • Salt, to taste
  • Pepper, to taste
  • 1 tablespoon of butter or oil
  • Optional fillings: cheese, diced vegetables, coocked meats, herbs

Instructions

1.

Crack the eggs: In a medium bowl, crack the eggs. Add a splash of milk (optional) for a slightly fluffier

2.

Whisk the eggs: Whisk the eggs with a fork or whisk until well combined and slightly frothy. Season with salt and pepper to taste..

3.

Heat a pan: Melt the butter or heat the olive oil in a non-stick pan over medium heat.

4.

Pour the egg mixture: Swirl the pan to coat the bottom with the melted butter/oil. Pour the egg mixture into the pan.

5.

Let it cook: Let the eggs cook for about 30 seconds to 1 minute, until the edges start to set.

6.

Add fillings (optional):I f you're using fillings, now is the time to add them. Sprinkle them evenly over the half of the omelette that is still uncooked.

7.

7. old the omelette: Using a spatula, gently fold one half of the omelette over the other half to enclose the fillings.

8.

Cook to desired doneness: Continue cooking for another 30 seconds to 1 minute, or until the omelette is cooked through to your preference.

9.

Slide onto plate and serve: Slide the omelette onto a plate and serve immediately. Enjoy!
